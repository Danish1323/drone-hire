Recognized Airspaces

Unrestricted Airspace

CLEARED FOR FLIGHT

Airspace up to 400 ft (120 m) that has not been designated as a red or yellow zone.

Altitude Limit 400 ft / 120m
DGCA Permission Not Required
Parameters
Flights are permitted without prior permission via the Digital Sky Platform.
Drone must still be registered with a valid UIN (Unique Identification Number).
Micro and Nano drones can fly freely; larger models require registered pilots.

Controlled Airspace

ATC CLEARANCE REQ

Controlled airspace requiring explicit prior permission from Air Traffic Control authorization entities.

Altitude Limit Varies (Usually 200ft)
ATC Permission MANDATORY
Parameters
Covers areas usually extending from 5km to 8km to 12km from the perimeter of operational airports.
Approval must be routed through the AAI (Airports Authority of India) and local ATC units.
A valid Flight Plan detailing time, vector, and altitude is mandatory.

Restricted Airspace

NO FLY ZONE

Strictly prohibited airspace covering vital installations, military bases, and international borders.

Standard Status GROUNDED
Clearance Entity Central Govt. / MoD
Parameters
Flying is absolutely prohibited under standard operational directives.
Only exceptional circumstances with clearance from the Central Government may permit flight.
Severe penal action and hardware seizure applies to violations natively.
